Privacy Policy

This is a starting template, not a substitute for legal advice. It has not been reviewed by a lawyer and should be before you rely on it for a real business — see the note at the bottom of this page.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information Sable Plumbing & Drain ("Sable," "we," "us") collects when you contact us for service, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it — including under Canada's federal privacy law (PIPEDA) and, for any visitors located in the EU/UK, the GDPR.

1. What we collect

InformationWhen
Name, phone number, service addressWhen you request a quote online, call us, or book a job
Email addressIf you provide one, for booking confirmations or receipts
Job details (what's wrong, urgency, photos if provided)To quote and schedule your service
Call recording & transcriptWhen you call our phone line, which is answered by an AI receptionist
Payment informationIf a deposit is required — handled entirely by our payment processor; we never see or store your card number
SMS consent recordWhether and when you agreed to receive text updates

2. How we use it

  • To quote, schedule, and dispatch a technician for your job
  • To send appointment confirmations and "on the way" text updates, only if you've agreed to receive them
  • To process a deposit or payment, if applicable
  • To follow up after a completed job (e.g. asking for a review), only if you've agreed to receive texts
  • To keep accounting and warranty records of work performed

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising unrelated to the service you requested.

3. Who we share it with

We use the following service providers to run our business. Each only receives the information needed to perform its function:

  • Twilio — sends text message updates, if you've consented
  • Stripe — processes deposit/payment transactions
  • Vapi / Anthropic — powers our AI phone receptionist, which processes and transcribes calls to our business line
  • Resend — delivers transactional emails (booking confirmations, review requests)
  • Supabase — hosts our database, physically located in Canada

We don't share your information with anyone else except where required by law (for example, in response to a valid legal request).

4. Where your data is stored

Our systems are hosted on infrastructure located in Canada. Some of the service providers above (Twilio, Stripe, Vapi, Anthropic, Resend) may process data on servers located outside Canada, including in the United States, as part of providing their service to us.

5. How long we keep it

We keep job and payment records as long as needed for accounting, warranty, and legal purposes — generally consistent with standard Canadian tax record-keeping requirements. We keep your contact information as long as you're an active or past customer, or until you ask us to delete it (see below).

6. Your rights & how to exercise them

You can ask us to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct information that's inaccurate or out of date
  • Delete your personal information, where we're not required to keep it for accounting, warranty, or legal reasons

We'll respond within 30 days, as required under PIPEDA (and, where applicable, the GDPR). You can also opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP, regardless of any other request.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encrypted connections, access controls, and reputable service providers) to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure; if we ever experience a breach that poses a real risk of harm, we'll notify affected individuals and, where required, the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, as required under PIPEDA.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are directed at adults arranging plumbing work for their home or business. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

10. Privacy Officer / contact us

Questions, concerns, or a request about your personal information? Contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@sableplumbing.example, or use the privacy request form. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.
