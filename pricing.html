The fine print, up front

Setup fee

$1,000–$2,000, charged upfront and non-refundable. Covers full onboarding — number porting, voice setup, your rate card, and dispatch configuration.

7-day free trial

Card required. The setup fee is charged immediately; your monthly fee is waived for the trial week. Keep going and monthly billing begins on day 8.

Seat limits

Enforced server-side. When you hire past a plan's limit, upgrade to the next tier in one click from Settings — no renegotiating, no downtime.

Try it free for 7 days

Pick a plan, port your number, and let Sable answer tonight. Cancel anytime during the trial and you owe only the setup.
