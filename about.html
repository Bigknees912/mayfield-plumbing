Why I built this

Founder, Sable

I watched a plumbing crew lose a $2,000 water heater job to a competitor who happened to pick up the phone first — while our guy was thirty seconds from calling the customer back. That's the whole problem. It's not that trade businesses are bad at the work. It's that the work doesn't stop long enough to answer the phone.

Sable exists to answer it for you: pick up, quote the job right, and get it on the calendar — so you're not choosing between finishing what's in front of you and losing what's next.

It started for plumbers because that's what I knew. It works the same way for electricians, HVAC crews, and locksmiths, because the actual problem — the phone doesn't stop ringing just because you're busy — is the same for all of them.
