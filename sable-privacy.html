Privacy Policy

This is a starting template, not a substitute for legal advice. It has not been reviewed by a lawyer and should be before you rely on it for a real business relationship with paying subscribers.

This Privacy Policy covers Sable's collection and use of information from the trade businesses that sign up for the platform, and from their team members. It does not cover the personal information a business collects from its own customers through the AI receptionist — each subscribing business publishes its own customer-facing privacy policy for that (Sable provides the tools; the subscriber decides how to use them and is responsible for its own customers' notices and consent).

1. What we collect from subscribers

InformationWhen
Business name, trade, service area, contact emailWhen you create a company account
Team member name, phone, email, roleWhen you or a team member joins via join code
Billing detailsHandled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card number
Usage data (jobs created, calls handled, feature usage)Ongoing, to operate and improve the platform

2. How we use it

  • To operate your account: authentication, seat/role enforcement, billing
  • To provide support when you contact us
  • To monitor platform health and fix bugs (error tracking via Sentry)
  • To improve Sable's features based on aggregate usage patterns

We do not sell subscriber information, and we do not use it to advertise unrelated products.

3. Who we share it with

We use the following service providers to run the platform. Each receives only the information needed to perform its function:

  • Supabase — hosts our database and authentication, in Canada
  • Stripe — processes subscription billing and job deposits
  • Vapi / Twilio / Anthropic — power the AI phone receptionist feature subscribers use with their own customers
  • Sentry — error monitoring, to help us catch and fix bugs

4. Where your data is stored

Our primary infrastructure is hosted in Canada. Some service providers above may process data on servers located outside Canada, including the United States, as part of providing their service to us.

5. Data retention

We keep your account and usage data for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period after cancellation for accounting and legal purposes, or until you ask us to delete it.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you as an account holder or team member, subject to accounting and legal retention requirements. Contact us using the details below; we'll respond within 30 days, consistent with PIPEDA.

7. Security

Subscriber data lives in a multi-tenant database with row-level security enforced at the database layer, so one company's data is never readable by another's, even in the event of a bug in our application code. We use encrypted connections and access controls throughout. No system is perfectly secure; if we experience a breach posing a real risk of harm, we'll notify affected accounts and, where required, the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@sable.example. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.
